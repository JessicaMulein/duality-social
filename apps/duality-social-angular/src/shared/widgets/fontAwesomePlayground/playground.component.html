  • The formatting follows three forms:
    1. {{iconName}} -> <i class="fa-regular fa-iconName"></i>
    2. {{iconStyle iconName}} -> <i class="fa-iconStyle fa-iconName"></i>
    3. {{iconStyle iconName ...style..}} -> <i class="fa-iconStyle fa-iconName" style="..style.."></i>
