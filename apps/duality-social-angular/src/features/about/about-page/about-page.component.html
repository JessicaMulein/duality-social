About

One of the weird political things that emerged since 2016 is the idea of everything needing to be presented with "both sides" of any issue. People said there need to be "both sides" of any issue, so here you can have it.

When OpenAI became available to us, the creator had the idea that it might be interesting to have a social network where every post is presented with a roughly opposite view and tone. So, if you post "I love bacon", the "other side" might be "I hate bacon".

This social network takes every post you make and presents it with the AI's idea of the Devil's Advocate view. Users then vote on which they think is the human's view (and logically the other is the AI's view).

There is also a separate reaction (like, love, hug, sad, etc) you can make use of.

This "social network" is an experiment, and an expensive one at that.

OpenAI is relatively expensive, in addition to other database, hosting, bandwidth, and other overhead necessary for Duality Social to function.

Duality Social is being presented as a Pay to Peek social experiment. We will create invitation codes with a limited number of uses, and you will be able to share those codes with your friends.

In order for this site to continue to exist, these things need to happen:

  • People need to use the site and enjoy it.
  • People need to share the site with their friends.
  • People need to pay enough for the site to at least break even.
  • Members need to remember to respect one another, and to follow the terms and rules we are bound to through our collective use of OpenAI.
  • Members need to report bad behavior immediately. Reports on a post or user will be reviewed by the site administrators and appropriate action will be taken as soon as possible.
  • * Reports on posts due to the AI's response may take down the post, but will not affect the user's account standing.
  • If the Peek funds are sufficient, additional moderation staff may be hired to help with the review process.
  • Members have a duty to report anything in breach of OpenAI's terms which will risk our ability to operate.
  • Be good, enjoy the mystery.

Front End

  • We're using Angular 15, Angular Material, and Angular Flex Layout, making RESTful calls to the backend to perform all operations.
  • We're also making extensive use of FontAwesome Pro, so you can use any of their icons, covered on this page and get to know the amazing selection of images you can include in anything you write.
  • See the Playground page to learn more about the supported formatting features, which includes generally github formatted markdown.

Back End

  • MongoDb

Above all, DualitySocial is Open Source.

Digital Defiance is a non-profit registered in Washington State.

Jessica Mulein is an employee of Microsoft, however this project is an independent work developed on private time on private hardware and made for the public good.

Duality Social was originally born with the monthly Azure play money we get as employees, and then we got a Microsoft grant which provided us ample resources. You can use the source code of this game to make another one that quickly integrates with Azure. As this work is open source and non-profit, it should not be any conflict of interest.

Ultimately, if the site costs more to exist than the grant money as well as the fees from OpenAI and any other overhead, it will have to be shut down unless donations are made. A formal donation process hasn't been established, but if you want to donate, please contact Jessica Mulein via email (jessica [at] digitaldefiance [dot] org).

Once the initial invitation period is over, usage will have to be covered by donations, like when you go to a museum and you have to pay to get in.
